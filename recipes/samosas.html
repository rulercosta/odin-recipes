Easy Baked Indian Samosas

These baked samosas have all the flavor and heartiness without frying. A healthier version that's just as delicious as traditional fried samosas.

Prep Time

25 minutes

Cook Time

35 minutes

Servings

16 samosas

Ingredients

  • 4 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 small onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tablespoons coriander seed
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 (1 inch) piece fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • ½ teaspoon ground allspice
  • ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 Roma (plum) tomatoes, finely chopped
  • ½ cup frozen peas
  • 2 (14.1 ounce) packages double-crust pie crusts, thawed
  • 2 large egg whites, beaten

Instructions

  1. Boil potatoes in salted water for 20 minutes until tender. Drain, mash coarsely, and set aside.
  2. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  3. Heat oil and sauté onions with spices for 5 minutes. Add tomatoes and peas, then mix with mashed potatoes. Let cool completely.
  4. Cut each pie crust into 8 triangles. Place filling at the wide end, fold corners over to form triangles, and pinch edges to seal.
  5. Brush with egg whites and bake for 15 minutes until golden brown.
