Vegetarian Korma

A rich, creamy vegetable curry with a blend of aromatic spices and cashew nuts.

Prep Time

20 minutes

Cook Time

35 minutes

Servings

4 people

Ingredients

  • 1½ tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
  • 4 carrots, cubed
  • 2 potatoes, cubed
  • 1 (4 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 3 tablespoons ground unsalted cashews
  • 1 fresh jalapeno pepper, seeded and sliced
  • 1½ tablespoons curry powder
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup frozen green peas
  • ½ green bell pepper, chopped
  • ½ red bell pepper, chopped
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add onion and cook until tender, about 2-3 minutes. Add garlic and ginger; cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Stir in carrots, potatoes, tomato sauce, cashews, and jalapeño. Season with curry powder and salt. Cook until potatoes are tender, about 10 minutes.
  4. Add cream, peas, and bell peppers. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 10 minutes. Garnish with cilantro to serve.
